Kerboodle Support Getting Started

The purchasing teacher receives an email from no-reply@kerboodle.com containing an account activitation link and login details. Clicking the link will activate the account. This link only works once.

The teacher invites the Administrator. The Administrator is the only person who can import student accounts onto Kerboodle. Each school only has one Administrator. View the Teacher walkthrough to learn how to invite the Administrator.

The Administrator imports all student accounts and invites other teachers to the platform. View the Administrator walkthrough.

Once the Administrator has imported the students, teachers can organise course access. There are two ways to do this:

  1. The first option is to create a code to give to your students. Students enter this code on their homepage to gain access.
  2. Alternatively, you can give access to an entire registration group as created by your Administrator.
View the Teacher walkthrough to learn how to organise course access.
