Kerboodle Support Moving students up a year

The Administrator archives any students who have left the school. For instructions on how to do this, view the section on archiving students in the Administrator walkthrough. Please do not delete your students.

The Administrator renames the current year groups. For example, Year 11 becomes Year 12. For instructions on how to do this, view the section on moving students up a year in the Administrator walkthrough.

The Administrator imports the new Year 7 students. For instructions on how to do this, view the section on creating users and groups in the Administrator walkthrough.

The teachers organise course access for the new year groups. For instructions on how to do this, view the User Management section of the Teacher walkthrough.
