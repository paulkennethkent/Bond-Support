Kerboodle Support Lessons FAQs


How do I find an item in the Lessons modules?

Use the folder structure on the left-and side of the screen. Clicking on one of these folders will filter the number of items in the main list to help you find the one you are looking for.

Alternatively, you can use the Search bar, and/or choose an option from the ‘Advanced’ menu to the right of the Search button.

Can I share content with my students?

Yes. You can create links within the Lessons module in Kerboodle to specific pieces of content, or collection of items, so that students don’t have to navigate the menus to find them.

How do I add my own content?

To add your own resources, click on Upload at the top left of your screen in the Lessons module. Browse to find the file on your computer or network. Choose a folder in which to place your item, or create a new folder.

You can find your own content by clicking on the My Plan filter.

If you want to make any changes, select the item using the tickbox, and click Preview above the list of content. Change the item’s information in the Preview pane that comes up.

Please note: you cannot add items that are over 5MB in size. Video and audio files cannot be uploaded.

How do I create my own content?

Click on Create at the top left of your screen to create your own content within this section of the course. Choose between Create plan and New/Edit folder.
