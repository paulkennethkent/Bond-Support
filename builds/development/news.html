May 1st

Use Kerboodle this exam season

Kerboodle is packed with tools to help you and your students through this exam season.

You can create your own lesson plans, resources and assessments to suit the revision needs of your students. Simply go to the relevant module within the course, click Create and use the tools available to create your materials.

You can also share useful resources with your students. Select your resources, click More and select Share content. You can then copy the link that appears into your VLE or in an email to your students.

The annotation tools in the Kerboodle Book are perfect for independent study. Encourage your students to use these in their revision at home.

Paul Kent

Apr 14th

Updates to Kerboodle

We made some updates to Kerboodle this morning to improve your user experience. We made the following changes:

  • It is now clearer to differentiate between test and support materials when an assignment is set.
  • The formatting has been improved when printing lesson plans. Click on the print icon in the top right hand corner in the Lesson view to print just that screen, and from the top right hand of the Notes tab to print all of the notes at the same time.
  • You can now easily scroll through the “My Courses” dropdown menu.

Lucy Huggett
